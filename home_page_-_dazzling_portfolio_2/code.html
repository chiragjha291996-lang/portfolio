The Problem Statement

Financial analysts were spending upwards of 15 hours per week manually sifting through thousands of unstructured documents—earnings call transcripts, 10-K filings, and market news—to generate a single comprehensive investment memo.

The existing keyword-based search tools were failing to capture semantic context, leading to missed insights and delayed decision-making in a high-frequency trading environment. The client needed a solution that could not only retrieve information but synthesize it into actionable intelligence.

hub

The Solution: Intelligent RAG Architecture

We implemented a sophisticated Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system. The core innovation was a multi-agent orchestration layer that decomposes complex analyst queries into sub-tasks (e.g., "retrieve 2023 revenue", "compare with 2022", "summarize risk factors").
